Victor Makarov             (ViktorMakarov@hotmail.com)

      

       1946:   Born in Volno-Chernikhovo (Brest region, Soviet Union, now Belarus).

 

      1968:   A master degree in mathematics from Minsk State University (Belarus).

 

      1967-1973: A software engineer at Novosibirsk (Russia).

                       (Computing Center of  Siberian Division of Russian Academy

                       of Sciences and some other places).

      

1973-1992: A senior research associate at Gomel Division of Institute of

        Mathematics of  Belarussian Academy of Sciences (BAN) (Gomel, Belarus).

 

1987:   A Ph. D. in Computer Science from the Institute of Mathematics of BAN (Minsk, Belarus).   

 

1992: Emigrated to USA (Brooklyn, New York).     

 

1993-current(2002): An independent software consultant, but also trying to finish a research

 project(design and implementation of a formal mathematical language) which I began before

 my emigration.
